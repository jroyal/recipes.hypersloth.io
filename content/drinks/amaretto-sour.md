---
author: "James Royal"
categories: ["drinks"]
tags: ["amaretto"]
title: "Amaretto Sour"
ingredients:
  - 2 oz amaretto
  - .75 oz lemon juice
  - .75 oz simple syrup
  - 1 egg white
instructions:
  - Dry shake all ingredients, then shake again with ice.
  - Double strain into a double rocks glass over 1 large ice cube.
---
