# Ingredients 
* 1.5 oz el Tesoro reposado tequila
* .5 oz mezcal
* 1 tsp agave nectar 
* 2 dashes angostura bitters
* 1 orange twist

# Instructions
Stir all ingredients over ice then strain into double rocks glass. 

To garnish, flame the orange twist over the drink and drop it in
