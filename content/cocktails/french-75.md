# Ingredients

* 1.5 oz gin
* .75 oz lemon juice
* .5 oz simple syrup
* Champagne 
* Lemon to garnish

# Instructions

Shake all except champagne. Pour into coupe and top with champagne. Spiral lemon twist and garnish.
