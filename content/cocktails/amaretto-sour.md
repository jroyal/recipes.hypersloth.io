# Ingredients

* 2 oz amaretto 
* .75 oz lemon juice
* .75 oz simple syrup
* 1 egg white
* Garnish: 1 dash angostura bitters and orange crescent

# Instructions

Dry shake all ingredients, then shake again with ice. Double strain into a double rocks glass over 1 large ice cube.
